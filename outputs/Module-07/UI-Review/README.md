## Navigation

- Sales Home: sign in with the Sales account; the focused Sales workspace opens automatically on `+ New Inquiry`.
- New Inquiry: click `+ New Inquiry` in the left navigation.
- AI Analysis: create the inquiry, then click `Analyze Inquiry` on the inquiry detail page.
- Quote Builder: select recommended products and click `Generate Quote`; to reopen later, click `Quotes`, then click the quote row.
- Orders: click `Orders` in the left navigation.
