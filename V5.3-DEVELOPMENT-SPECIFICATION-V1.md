## 3. Core Development Principle

Do not rebuild the current system.

The correct path is:

```text
Restaurant Setup Pro Existing System
        ↓
AI Sales OS V5.3 Lite
        ↓
Future SaaS Architecture
```

Existing completed foundations must be reused:

- Product Library
- Product Intelligence
- Product Knowledge Engine
- AI Product Content Factory
- Opportunity Intelligence Engine
- Sales Intelligence Workflow
- Quote Builder
- PI Builder
- Product Price Engine
- AI Cost Control Framework

## 4. Single Source of Truth Rules

Future modules must not duplicate core business data.

| Business Data | Source of Truth |
| --- | --- |
| Product data | Product Library / Product Intelligence Center |
| Product knowledge | Product Knowledge Engine |
| Product variants / attributes | Product Foundation / PIM |
| Supplier cost and reference price | Product Price Engine |
| Customer and opportunity data | Opportunity Intelligence |
| Inquiry and sales workflow | Sales Intelligence |
| Quote calculation | Quote Builder |
| Customer-facing PI | PI Builder |
| AI budget and provider control | AI Cost Control |

AI modules may store:

- Drafts
- Recommendations
- Analysis results
- Snapshots
- Logs
- Review records

AI modules must not create duplicate product/customer/quote master records unless the user-approved workflow explicitly applies the result into the source-of-truth module.

---

## 5. AI Governance Rules

All AI work must follow these rules:

1. AI output is advisory by default.
2. AI-generated business content must be reviewable.
3. AI must not automatically send external messages.
4. AI must not silently change prices.
5. AI must not overwrite Product Library data without human approval.
6. AI must not expose supplier cost to unauthorized roles.
7. All paid AI provider calls must pass through AI Cost Control.
8. Batch AI operations require estimated cost and user confirmation.
9. Mock/rules provider must remain available as safe fallback.
10. AI logs must not expose API keys or secrets.

## 7. Required Development Phases

Future V5.3 development should follow this order unless the user explicitly changes priority.

### Phase 0 — Architecture Preparation

Prepare shared contracts, context builder design, provider adapter design, module ownership, and no-code architecture documents.

### Phase 1 — AI Business Brain

Create the shared AI orchestration layer, AI context builder, action registry, provider execution contract, logs, and Debug Center visibility.

### Phase 2 — AI Lead Discovery Agent

Add lead discovery batch foundation and connector-ready design for Google Maps, Apollo, website analysis, and customer signal detection.

### Phase 3 — Opportunity Intelligence Upgrade

Improve scoring, enrichment, signals, product matching, duplicate detection, outreach drafting, and sales handoff.

### Phase 4 — AI Solution Generator

Generate human-reviewed furniture package drafts using Product Intelligence, customer context, budget, store type, style, and quote readiness.

### Phase 5 — AI Pricing Intelligence

Explain and review pricing risks while keeping deterministic price calculation inside Product Price Engine.

### Phase 6 — AI PI Generator Upgrade

Improve PI/customer message generation, PI quality checks, remarks, payment/freight wording, and human-reviewed customer-facing output.

### Phase 7 — Sales Dashboard

Create a practical daily sales operating dashboard showing priority leads, follow-ups, quote risks, AI recommendations, and next actions.

### Phase 8 — SaaS Preparation

Plan tenant isolation, billing, feature flags, storage migration, role normalization, background jobs, and SaaS admin architecture.
