# Restaurant Setup Pro AI Sales OS V5.3

# Integration & Upgrade Plan v1.0

Date: 2026-07-09  
Target industry: Hospitality Furniture / Restaurant Furniture  
Planning scope: Existing Restaurant Setup Pro system → AI Sales OS V5.3 Lite → Future SaaS Architecture  
Implementation rule: Do not rebuild the current system. Reuse and extend existing foundations.

---

## 0. Executive Summary

Restaurant Setup Pro already contains a substantial business system foundation:

- Product Library
- Product Intelligence
- Product Knowledge Engine
- AI Product Content Factory
- Opportunity Intelligence Engine
- Sales Intelligence Workflow
- Quote Builder
- PI Builder
- Product Price Engine
- AI Cost Control Framework

AI Sales OS V5.3 should not replace these modules. It should connect them into a higher-level AI sales operating layer.

The correct upgrade path is:

```text
Restaurant Setup Pro Existing System
        ↓
AI Sales OS V5.3 Lite
        ↓
Future SaaS Architecture
```

The existing system is strongest in:

- Product data foundation
- Supplier Excel import
- Quote / PI workflow
- Product pricing foundation
- Opportunity scoring foundation
- AI cost governance

The missing V5.3 capabilities are mainly:

- AI Lead Discovery
- Real LLM provider layer
- Semantic knowledge retrieval
- AI Business Brain orchestration
- AI solution/proposal generation
- AI learning loop
- SaaS-ready modular architecture

### 1.2 Reuse Principle

AI Sales OS V5.3 should follow these reuse rules:

1. Product data remains in Product Library / Product Intelligence.
2. Customer and opportunity data remains in Opportunity Intelligence.
3. Quotes and PI remain deterministic business documents.
4. AI does not overwrite official business records without review.
5. AI suggestions are stored as drafts, analysis records, or recommendations.
6. Cost Control wraps every AI execution.
7. Future modules call existing APIs instead of copying data.

### 2.3 AI Intelligence Layer

#### AI Business Brain

Purpose:

- Central reasoning layer for hospitality furniture sales.
- Coordinates customer, product, pricing, quote, PI, and opportunity context.
- Produces recommendations, not uncontrolled record changes.

Responsibilities:

- Build AI context from Product + Customer + Quote data.
- Decide which specialized AI workflow to call.
- Enforce human review and cost control.
- Store analysis outputs and learning signals.

#### AI Lead Discovery Agent

Purpose:

- Find and qualify potential restaurant/hospitality furniture leads.

Future sources:

- Google Maps
- Apollo
- Website lists
- Manual CSV
- Public website analysis
- Social links

Responsibilities:

- Discover candidate businesses.
- Extract contact/company/store signals.
- Detect business type, location, expansion/renovation/furniture need.
- Create draft customer records for review.

#### Opportunity Intelligence

Purpose:

- Score, grade, enrich, and prioritize leads.

Existing foundation:

- Module 06A already implements scoring, gaps, recommendations, drafts, and handoff.

V5.3 upgrade:

- Consume Lead Discovery output.
- Add richer signals.
- Use semantic product matching.
- Generate better next actions.

#### AI Solution Generator

Purpose:

- Convert a customer need into a recommended furniture package.

Inputs:

- Customer profile
- Inquiry text
- Business type
- Store style
- Budget level
- Product Intelligence
- Price constraints
- Past cases

Outputs:

- Recommended categories
- Recommended products/variants
- Custom item suggestions
- Missing information checklist
- Draft package notes

Rule:

- It creates a draft solution. Sales must approve before quote/PI.

#### AI Pricing Intelligence

Purpose:

- Help sales and owner understand price/margin risk.

Responsibilities:

- Explain reference price source.
- Flag missing cost, missing rule, outdated exchange rate, low margin risk.
- Suggest price rule review.
- Compare Economy / Standard / Premium package scenarios.

Rule:

- Final price calculation remains deterministic in Price Engine.
- AI only explains and recommends.

#### AI Proposal / PI Intelligence

Purpose:

- Upgrade Quote/PI into smarter customer-facing packages.

Responsibilities:

- Generate PI remarks.
- Generate payment/freight wording.
- Generate email/WhatsApp message.
- Check missing specs/images/prices.
- Suggest customer-facing product descriptions.
- Later generate full proposal pages.

Rule:

- AI should never overwrite PI totals.

#### AI Sales Assistant

Purpose:

- Daily sales copilot.

Responsibilities:

- Show who to contact today.
- Explain why a lead matters.
- Recommend next message.
- Summarize customer history.
- Suggest product package.
- Warn missing data before quote/PI.

V5.3 Lite behavior:

- Assistant can produce draft recommendations and messages.
- Sales approves every external message.

#### AI Learning Loop

Purpose:

- Improve recommendations over time from business outcomes.

Signals:

- Quote accepted/rejected
- Product selected
- Price adjusted
- Customer replied
- Order won/lost
- Missing product data corrected
- Import errors corrected

Outputs:

- Better scoring
- Better product matching
- Better templates
- Better prompts
- Product knowledge gap list

## 3. Missing Capability Analysis

### A. AI Lead Discovery

Current state:

- Customer import and Opportunity Intelligence exist.
- Manual/CSV/bulk import exists.
- External discovery is not implemented.

Needed capabilities:

#### Google Maps Integration Possibility

Use cases:

- Discover restaurants, coffee shops, bubble tea shops, bakeries, bars, hotels, food courts by city/region.
- Extract business name, address, phone, website, rating, category, store signals.

Required design:

- Connector layer, not direct business-code calls.
- Store source URL and confidence.
- Respect API terms, rate limits, and cost controls.
- Create draft leads, not final sales-ready records.

#### Apollo Integration Possibility

Use cases:

- Find decision makers.
- Enrich company/contact data.
- Identify owner, purchasing, operations, founder, designer roles.

Required design:

- Contact enrichment job.
- Source confidence and timestamp.
- Deduplication before insert.
- Human review for imported contacts.

#### Website Analysis

Use cases:

- Analyze restaurant website for brand style, number of locations, expansion signals, renovation signals, menu/store concept.
- Detect suitable furniture package direction.

Required design:

- Website fetch/analyze connector.
- Store website snapshot or analysis summary.
- Do not scrape uncontrolled large content.
- Cost Control and cache required.

#### Customer Signal Detection

Signals:

- New store opening
- Multi-location / chain
- Renovation language
- Franchise / expansion
- Outdoor seating
- New menu/concept launch
- Job posts suggesting expansion
- Social media construction/opening posts

Output:

- `expansion_probability`
- `renovation_probability`
- `furniture_need_probability`
- `next_action`
- `recommended_sales_angle`

## 6. Risk Analysis

### 6.1 Avoid Duplicate Development

Risk:

- New AI modules may recreate product, customer, quote, or pricing data.

Prevention:

- Product Library remains product source of truth.
- Opportunity Intelligence remains customer/opportunity source of truth.
- Quote Builder remains commercial calculation source of truth.
- Price Engine remains pricing source of truth.
- AI stores drafts, snapshots, logs, and recommendations only.

### 6.2 Avoid Breaking Existing Modules

Risk:

- AI Sales OS work could disrupt working Product Library, Import Center, Quote Builder, or PI Builder.

Prevention:

- Use additive migrations.
- Add new endpoints instead of changing existing endpoints.
- Keep existing UI workflow stable.
- Require regression tests for:
  - Product Library
  - Import Center
  - Quote Builder
  - PI Export
  - Opportunity Intelligence
  - AI Cost Control

### 6.3 AI Cost Control

Risk:

- Lead discovery, website analysis, and LLM generation can create uncontrolled AI/API cost.

Prevention:

- Every AI run goes through AI Cost Control.
- Batch operations require estimate and confirmation.
- Default provider remains mock/rules.
- Paid providers require Admin/Owner activation.
- Cache repeated customer/product analysis.
- Per-module and future per-tenant budgets.

### 6.4 Future SaaS Scalability

Risk:

- Current single-tenant internal architecture may become hard to convert later.

Prevention:

- Introduce tenant model by plan before public SaaS launch.
- Avoid hard-coded organization-wide assumptions.
- Normalize roles.
- Move media to durable object storage.
- Introduce background jobs for imports, AI runs, exports.
- Add observability and tenant-level audit logs.

### 6.5 AI Quality and Trust

Risk:

- AI may hallucinate product details, prices, lead signals, or promises.

Prevention:

- AI context must come from official source tables.
- AI cannot calculate final price.
- AI cannot send messages automatically in V5.3 Lite.
- AI output requires human review before Apply.
- Store source references and confidence.

### 6.6 Data Privacy and Sensitive Supplier Information

Risk:

- Supplier cost and purchasing information could leak to sales/customer-facing AI output.

Prevention:

- Continue server-side redaction.
- AI context builder must respect role and purpose.
- Customer-facing AI context excludes supplier cost unless explicitly approved for pricing review.
- Logs must avoid secrets and API keys.

- Price Engine

- Add AI Business Brain.

- Add solution generation.
