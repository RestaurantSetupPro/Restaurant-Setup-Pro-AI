# Restaurant Setup Pro AI Sales OS V5.3

# Product Intelligence Center
# Data Model & Upgrade Specification V1.0


## Objective

Upgrade the current Product Import Center into an AI Product Intelligence Center.

The goal:

Transform any supplier/product data source into a standardized B2B product knowledge system.

The system should support:

- Product management
- AI product understanding
- Customer-product matching
- Quotation generation
- PI generation
- Sales recommendation


---

# 1. Core Principle


Input:

Any supplier data format.


Supported:

- XLSX
- CSV
- Product images
- Product catalogs
- Future PDF documents


Important:

Do not require suppliers to use a fixed template.


AI should identify:

- Columns
- Product fields
- Attributes
- Images
- Variants
- Pricing information


Then map into a unified product model.


---

# 2. Product Master Model


Every product should have:


## Basic Information

Fields:

- Product ID
- Product Name
- SKU
- Category
- Brand
- Product Status
- Main Image


---

# 3. Product Attribute Model


Support dynamic attributes.


Examples:


Furniture:

- Material
- Color
- Size
- Style
- Finish


Equipment:

- Capacity
- Power
- Specification
- Certification


The system should not hard-code attributes.


---

# 4. Variant Model


Products may contain multiple variants.


Example:


Product:

Restaurant Chair


Variants:


- Black PU
- Brown PU
- Fabric Grey


Each variant:

- SKU
- Image
- Size
- Cost
- Selling Price


---

# 5. Commercial Data Model


Internal business information:


## Supplier Cost

Fields:

- Supplier Price
- Currency
- Exchange Rate


## Pricing Rules

Support:

- Distributor pricing
- Restaurant owner pricing
- Project pricing


Formula:

Cost + Margin Rule = Selling Price
