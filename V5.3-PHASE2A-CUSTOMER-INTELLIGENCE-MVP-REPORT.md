## 3. API Changes

New APIs:

- `POST /api/customers/:id/customer-intelligence/run`
  - Runs Phase 2A Customer Intelligence scoring.
  - Supports manual intelligence input.
  - Uses deterministic rules first.
  - Calls AI Business Brain for score explanation logging.
  - Stores reviewable profile and score history.

- `POST /api/customers/:id/customer-intelligence/feedback`
  - Stores sales feedback for future learning preparation.
  - Does not train or automatically adjust scoring weights.

- `GET /api/customer-intelligence/priority`
  - Returns customers sorted by Sales Priority Score.

Updated APIs:

- `GET /api/customers/:id`
  - Now includes latest intelligence profiles, feedback, and score history.

- `GET /api/debug/system`
  - Opportunity Intelligence debug data now includes Customer Intelligence profile count, feedback count, score history count, and last Customer Intelligence run.

## 6. Known Limitations

- Phase 2A does not implement external lead discovery.
- No Apollo, Google Maps, LinkedIn, website crawler, email automation, or autonomous agent was added.
- Scoring weights are deterministic MVP rules, not a full configurable industry template engine yet.
- AI provider remains mock/rules through AI Business Brain.
- AI does not automatically adjust scoring weights.
- Feedback is stored for future learning preparation but does not train a model.
- Customer Intelligence profiles are stored as draft/reviewable records; full approval workflow can be expanded in Phase 2B.
