# V5.3 Opportunity Intelligence V3.2.2 CRM Workflow Refactor Report

## Objective

Refactor the Opportunity Intelligence workflow so Sales users can clearly understand the difference between:

- Lead
- Customer

This update does not add new AI capability, database tables, API endpoints, external integrations, or automation.

## Workflow Refactor

Updated conceptual flow:

Search Task
→ Search Results
→ Lead Pool
→ Lead Detail
→ Convert to Customer
→ Customers CRM
→ Customer Detail
→ Sales Pipeline

## UI / Navigation Changes

Opportunity Intelligence navigation now emphasizes:

- AI Discovery
- Search Tasks
- Lead Pool
- Customers
- Priority View

The previous “Customer List” naming was replaced with clearer CRM terminology:

- Lead Pool: pre-customer discovery and qualification workspace
- Customers: CRM records after conversion

## Lead Pool

Added a Lead Pool view based on existing Search Results.

Lead Pool shows:

- Lead company
- Source
- Location
- Customer type
- Opportunity score
- Purchase potential
- AI recommendation
- Evidence
- Status
- Open action

## Lead Detail

Open now goes to Lead Detail for Search Results / Leads.

Lead Detail shows:

- AI Summary
- Customer Evidence
- Website
- Contact
- AI Recommendation
- Product Matching
- Customer Intelligence explanation
- Update Intelligence action
- Activity History
- Convert to Customer
- Discard

## Convert to Customer

Convert is now only exposed in Lead Detail.

Search Results tables no longer show direct Convert Customer buttons.

When converted:

- Lead history remains.
- Customer record is created using existing conversion logic.
- Customer can be opened from the converted lead.

## Customer Page Responsibility

Customers page is now positioned as CRM after conversion.

It remains responsible for:

- Sales follow-up
- Customer detail
- Customer intelligence update
- Quotes / PI / order-related sales workflows

It no longer carries the “Customer List” discovery wording.
